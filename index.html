About

Selected scholarly & technical work

Course research · unpublished

Federated learning in smart cities

Wrote a research paper for Advanced Computer Networks on applying federated learning in smart-city settings (privacy-preserving aggregation, communication constraints). Manuscript not formally published; available on request.

ML course project

Reinforcement learning for navigation

Implemented a reinforcement-learning agent that learns obstacle avoidance and goal reaching through a reward signal in a grid-style environment, connecting policy learning to sequential decision making.

2026 · comparative study

Spiking neural networks vs. classical baselines

Built an RBF-kernel SVM baseline on Fashion-MNIST (≈90.02% test accuracy, 90.00% macro F1) and analyzed trade-offs versus spiking architectures, including input sparsity and energy-style considerations at the edge.

2026 · multimodal AI

Context-aware NPC storytelling

End-to-end pipeline from gameplay screenshots: a vision–language model extracts structured game state; a large language model generates context-aware NPC narrative conditioned on that state.

Competition · Nov 2025

Tree canopy instance segmentation (Solafune)

Geospatial instance segmentation for urban tree canopy; tiled large rasters, handled thousands of polygon instances in Python, and optimized for weighted mAP under difficult resolution and clutter. Team placed 38th on the leaderboard.

Embedded vision

Parking occupancy detector (Raspberry Pi)

Object-detection style pipeline to infer whether vehicles occupied spaces in a ~10-car lot at roughly 25m distance; reported ≈92% success rate in field tests for the IEEE / workshop context.
